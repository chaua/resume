Profile

Hello, I'm Chauã! I hold a master's degree in Computer Science from the Federal University of Paraná (UFPR) with a specialization in Digital Image Processing and Computer Vision.

Currently, I work as a professor in the Bachelor's program in Computer Science and Technology in Systems Analysis and Development at Tuiuti do Paraná University (UTP), where I teach courses in Computational Intelligence, Digital Image Processing, Advanced Programming and Mobile Device Programming. Working in an academic environment is very rewarding because of the freedom to study different subjects and technologies, as well as the opportunity to share experiences with students from different backgrounds.

In addition to teaching, I have more than 10 years of professional experience with multinational tech companies such as HSBC Global Technology (GLT) and Capgemini. I currently work in the technology, data and Business Intelligence area of RPC, Rede Globo's affiliated television station in Paraná. My responsibilities there include creating mobile and web systems, surveying the needs of the company's business areas, checking functional requirements, systems documentation, data modeling, analysis and modification of processes, and project implementation and maintenance.
